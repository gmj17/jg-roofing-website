Roof Repairs & Leak Work

Repair work starts with source diagnosis. We focus on why the leak started so fixes last longer.

Common Repair Types

  • Flashing and valley failures
  • Pipe boots and penetration leaks
  • Storm-lifted shingles/tiles
  • Fastener and seam issues on metal roofs

Material-Specific Repairs

  • Shingle: replace damaged tabs and reseal vulnerable rows
  • Tile: replace broken tiles and restore underlayment protection
  • Metal: reseal transitions and correct panel fasteners/seams

Repair Process

  • Leak tracing and moisture check
  • Photo-documented findings
  • Targeted fix with matching materials
  • Post-repair verification
