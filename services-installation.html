Installation Process by Material

Shingle Process

  • Deck inspection and prep
  • Water barrier and underlayment install
  • Starter strips, field shingles, and ridge venting
  • Flashing and edge metal detailing

Tile Process

  • Structural suitability review
  • Enhanced underlayment and battens
  • Tile layout for drainage and uplift resistance
  • Ridge/hip closure and flashing details

Metal Process

  • Substrate leveling and moisture barrier
  • Panel layout and secure fastening plan
  • Seam locking and transition detailing
  • Final seal checks around penetrations

Final Handoff

  • Photo documentation of key stages
  • Clean-up and walkthrough
  • Maintenance recommendations by material
  • Warranty and care instructions

When Roof Replacement Makes Sense

Full replacement restores system integrity when repairs are no longer cost-effective.

Signs You Need Replacement

  • Widespread leaks across multiple slopes
  • Aging roof near end-of-life
  • Repeated repairs with recurring failures
  • Deck or underlayment deterioration

Replacement Benefits

  • Restore full weather protection
  • Upgrade to better materials
  • Eliminate recurring repair costs
  • Improve home value and insurance rates

Replacement Workflow

  • Inspection and scope definition
  • Tear-off and deck evaluation
  • Install new waterproofing layers
  • Install selected roofing material
