Installation Process

1. Pre-Installation Inspection

  • Comprehensive roof evaluation
  • Structural assessment and condition report
  • Identify any underlying issues
  • Take photos and document findings

2. Material Selection & Planning

  • Review available material options
  • Discuss performance and durability
  • Confirm budget and timeline
  • Material-specific details reviewed separately

3. Permitting & Scheduling

  • Obtain required permits and inspections
  • Coordinate scheduling with your calendar
  • Notify your insurance company
  • Final walkthrough before work begins

4. Professional Installation & Handoff

  • Expert installation by licensed crew
  • Final inspection of all work
  • Complete clean-up and site restoration
  • Warranty documentation and care instructions
