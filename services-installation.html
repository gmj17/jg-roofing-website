Roof Installation

New installation planning starts with structure, climate, and long-term cost. We install systems based on material behavior in Florida heat, rain, and wind.

Material Types We Install

Shingle Roofing

Cost-effective and versatile with strong wind-rated options and easy future serviceability.

  • Popular architectural shingle profiles
  • Fast installation timeline
  • Great value for most residential homes

Tile Roofing

Premium curb appeal with long lifespan and strong heat resistance when structure supports the load.

  • Concrete and clay tile systems
  • Excellent durability in sun exposure
  • Higher upfront cost, long-term value

Metal Roofing

High-performance option for wind and rain with long service life and low maintenance.

  • Standing seam and screw-down profiles
  • Strong uplift and weather performance
  • Energy-efficient reflective finishes

Installation Process by Material

Shingle Process

  • Deck inspection and prep
  • Water barrier and underlayment install
  • Starter strips, field shingles, and ridge venting
  • Flashing and edge metal detailing

Tile Process

  • Structural suitability review
  • Enhanced underlayment and battens
  • Tile layout for drainage and uplift resistance
  • Ridge/hip closure and flashing details

Metal Process

  • Substrate leveling and moisture barrier
  • Panel layout and secure fastening plan
  • Seam locking and transition detailing
  • Final seal checks around penetrations

Final Handoff

  • Photo documentation of key stages
  • Clean-up and walkthrough
  • Maintenance recommendations by material
  • Warranty and care instructions
