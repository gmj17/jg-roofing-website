Inspection Services

Our inspections are built to reduce risk and help you make smart roofing decisions quickly.

What We Inspect

Critical Roof Components

  • Shingle/tile displacement and surface wear
  • Metal panel uplift, fasteners, and seam integrity
  • Flashing transitions, valleys, and penetrations
  • Interior indicators of active or past water intrusion
Decision Clarity

Report + Action Plan

  1. Document: Photo-backed findings by roof area
  2. Prioritize: Clear severity and urgency notes
  3. Recommend: Repair vs replacement guidance
  4. Plan: Next steps aligned to timeline and budget

Maintenance Services

Preventative care protects your investment, extends roof lifespan, and reduces surprise repair costs.

Material-Specific Care

Maintenance by Roof Type

  • Tile: cracked tile replacement and underlayment watchpoints
  • Metal: seam, fastener, and coating condition monitoring
Service Program
