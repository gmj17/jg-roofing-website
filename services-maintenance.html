Roof Inspection & Maintenance

Inspection identifies issues, maintenance prevents them. Keep your roof performing with proactive care.

Call

Inspection Services

We document roof conditions with clear findings that help you plan repairs and prevent future issues.

What We Inspect

  • Shingle/tile displacement and loss
  • Metal panel uplift and seam integrity
  • Flashing transitions and penetrations
  • Interior indicators of water intrusion

Report Quality

  • Photo-backed findings by roof area
  • Clear severity and urgency notes
  • Repair vs replacement guidance
  • Prioritized next steps

Inspection Process

  • On-site walkthrough and visual survey
  • Critical-point condition capture
  • Written summary with recommendations
  • Follow-up planning call if needed

Maintenance Services

Preventative maintenance extends roof life by catching failures early, before they become expensive leak events.

Maintenance by Material

  • Shingle: granule loss, seal strip and edge checks
  • Tile: cracked tile replacement and underlayment watchpoints
  • Metal: seam, fastener, and coating condition checks

Program Inclusions

  • Scheduled condition reviews
  • Minor corrective adjustments
  • Photo logs for trend tracking
  • Seasonal storm-readiness checks

Maintenance Cycle

  • Baseline inspection and checklist
  • Routine follow-up visits
  • Priority repair recommendations
  • Annual planning summary
