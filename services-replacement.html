Roof Replacement

When repairs are no longer cost-effective, full replacement restores system integrity and reduces recurring leak risk.

When Replacement Makes Sense

  • Widespread leaks across multiple slopes
  • Aging roof near end-of-life
  • Repeated repairs with recurring failures
  • Deck or underlayment deterioration

Material Upgrade Options

  • Shingle: balanced cost and performance
  • Tile: high-end look and long lifespan
  • Metal: strong wind/rain performance

Replacement Workflow

  • Inspection and scope definition
  • Tear-off and deck evaluation
  • Install new waterproofing layers
  • Install selected roofing material
